─── FULL DESCRIPTION (≤200 words) ───────────────────────────
Mosquitoes are invading — and they're evolving!

Click to swat them before the swarm grows out of control.
Five types of mosquitoes keep you on your toes:

• Normal  — slow but they evolve into Fast if you ignore them
• Fast    — zippy and worth double blood
• Stealth — flicker invisible; you can't always see them!
• Mutant  — tanky 2-HP bruisers
• Boss    — a massive, sprint-charging monster arrives every 60s

Earn Blood Drops by killing mosquitoes, then spend them in the
Alchemy Shop between (or during) runs on permanent upgrades
that carry across every session:

⚡ Bug Zapper — AoE lightning strike
💣 Mosquito Bomb — instant screen-clear
🛡 Bite Shield — 10-second invincibility
❄ Freeze Spray — stop all mosquitoes cold
🧲 Blood Magnet — auto-collect all blood drops
🌀 Incense Coil — passive auto-kill every few seconds
...and more!

Three difficulty modes (Normal / Hard / Hell) let beginners
enjoy the game while veterans chase the S-rank leaderboard.
How long can you survive?
